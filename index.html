Jordan Borth

User Interface Designer

I design interfaces for digital products, with a focus on tools and utilities for the Mac and iOS. I strive to design products that get the details right, that feel great to use and that meet my own meticulous standards and expectations. I leave my ego at the door, research and talk with people, share early and often and constantly iterate in order to arrive at the best solution possible.

I believe in less, but better and that simple and intentional design improves our lives.

I'm a UI designer at Quill where we believe messaging built for productivity, not engagement, is the future of real‑time work communication.

In the past, I’ve had the pleasure of working with great companies and clients including: MetaLab, Square, Flinto, AppZapper, BuddyBuild and CloudApp.
